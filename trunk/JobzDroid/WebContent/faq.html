FAQ

How does the posting procedure work for job posters?

First, log-in with your employer account, and click on the "New Ad" button under the heading "My Ads", or inside the side menu bar on the right side of the window. Fill the Ad details form, and then click on "Post it" button under the heading "Create New Ad".

Done! Your new Ad should now have a "Pending" status, and submitted to the Administrator for approval. Once approved, the Ad status will be changed to "Open", and it will become visible to the public.
